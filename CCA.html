Not All White Walls
2019 CCA Commencement Exhibition

The design for the 2019 CCA Commencement Exhibition showcases a wide range of work from different creative disciplines while maintaining a cohesive attitude towards the space, a former Greyhound Bus maintenance garage converted into an academic building in 1999. The four immersively colorful rooms that punctuate the space not only mitigate the grand scale of the existing architecture and ease wayfinding but also function as thresholds between the central nave and smaller exhibition rooms, resulting in a surprisingly intimate viewing experience given the overall scale of the space.

Type of Project: Exhibition Design
Location: San Francisco, CA
Completion Date: May 2019
