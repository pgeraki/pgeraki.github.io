PRACTICE
PALMYRA PLLC is a woman-owned WBE-certified full-service architecture practice based in Chicago.
Architecture always starts with a problem: sometimes an eminently practical one and sometimes an abstract or conceptual one. Beyond the clinical joy of problem-solving there is also something deeply rewarding to the process of listening to the needs and desires of a client/end user and helping them realize their vision by infusing it with the spatial thinking, holistic planning, and technical expertise only an architect can provide. Architecture, after all, is a deeply humanistic endeavor, one that requires empathy, conviction, and an insatiable appetite for knowledge, the knowledge that comes with inhabiting a problem, internalizing its limitations, and making the judgment calls that ultimately lead to its solution. In our work we strive to adopt this exacting but empathic approach to design.
PRINCIPAL
Palmyra Geraki is a practicing architect and educator. She is the founding principal of Palmyra PLLC. Palmyra is a licensed architect in the United States (Illinois - 001.025033, New York - 037113) and Greece.
Before founding her own practice, Palmyra worked on affordable housing and other mission-driven projects as Project Manager/Project Architect at Leddy Maytum Stacy Architects in San Francisco and on a wide range of mid-rise and high-rise residential and commercial projects as an Associate at Pei Cobb Freed & Partners in New York City.
Palmyra teaches at the University of Illinois in Chicago. She previously taught at California College of the Arts in San Francisco and has frequently been an invited juror at Yale, IIT, UC Berkeley, Pratt, City College of New York, New Jersey Institute of Technology, California College of the Arts, Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México, Texas A&M, University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee, and University of Illinois at Chicago.
Palmyra received her B.A. in 'Architecture' and 'Ethics, Politics & Economics' from Yale University and her M.Arch. from the Yale School of Architecture, where she was the recipient of the George Nelson Traveling Fellowship. She grew up in Thessaloniki, Greece, and has lived in New Haven, New York, the Bay Area, and Chicago.
PREVIOUS PROJECTS
Firm: Pei Cobb Freed & Partners Architects
Role: Project Architect
Client: Next Century Associates, LLC
Location: Los Angeles, CA
Status: Completed
Project Description: The master plan for this project, redesigned several times through the years to better align with existing urban axes and corridors, responded to complex site conditions and calls from local preservationists by preserving the existing Century Plaza hotel on site. Currently under construction, the twin residential towers of the new Century Plaza development complete the axis created by Minoru Yamasaki’s iconic twin towers and historic crescentshaped hotel as well as Gensler’s more recent 2000 Avenue of the Stars.
The 1-million-square-foot new development, which includes two residential towers with condominium units, amenities, restaurants, retail space, landscaped parks, plazas, and onsite parking, involved a team of four architectural offices, an interior design firm, and over thirty consultants, sub-contractors, and specialized owner's representatives. Overlapping scopes of work and a shift to a design-build during Design Development rendered the process of coordination difficult but ultimtaely rewarding.
