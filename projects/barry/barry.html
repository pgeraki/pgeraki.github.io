THREE-FLAT ETUDE
The south- and street-facing facade of this new 4-story building in Chicago's Lakeview neighborhood is a playful take on the omnipresent porch.
The building will sit on a typical Chicago lot 25' wide by 120' deep and features 4 residential units and a large occupiable rooftop.
Type of Project: New Construction, Multi-family
Location: Chicago, IL
