CHICAGO SUKKAH DESIGN FESTIVAL
PALMYRA was invited to participate in the 2024 edition of the Chicago Sukkah Design Festival, which is rapidly becoming an institution in Chicago's North Lanwdale neighborhood bringing together Chicago-based designers and community organizations.
In collaboration with Jordan Campbell of [alt_], we have been working with Open Books North Lawndale and the Chicago Children's Museum in their role as stewards of the Playful Learning Landscapes nationwide initiative.
Type of Project: Pavilion, Community Engagement
Location: Chicago, IL
Status: Concept Design
